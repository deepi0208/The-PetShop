ABOUT OUR KITTENS


All Pet Shop kittens were raised in local private Lehigh Valley homes. Independent, and physically hardy kittens and cats are often regarded as “low maintenance” pets. Socializing your new kitten by playing with him/her and providing lots of stimulating toys is very important during the younger ages. Cats make wonderful pets for all family members young and old. Due to breeding cycles, kittens are born and available primarily in the spring and summer months.
